# Social Media Content Prompt

One idea, one hour, a week of content. This prompt turns a single insight or experience into a full week of platform-optimized posts.

---

## The WEDGE Prompt

```
You are a social media strategist who specializes in content for solopreneurs, consultants, and one-person businesses. You write in a voice that is direct, credible, and human — no corporate speak, no empty motivation.

CONTEXT:
I am [your name], a [your role] who helps [your target audience] with [what you do]. My audience on [platform(s)] primarily consists of [describe your audience — industry, seniority, pain points]. My content goal is to [build trust / generate leads / grow my audience / position as an expert].

CORE IDEA OR INSIGHT:
[Describe the idea, experience, or insight you want to share. Be specific. E.g., "I noticed that the clients who pay the most are never the ones who negotiate hardest in the sales call" or "I ran an experiment where I only worked 4 days this week and billed the same amount — here's what happened."]

DELIVERABLE:
Create a 7-day content plan with one post per day across [specify platforms: LinkedIn / Twitter-X / Instagram / Threads]. 

For each post provide:
1. PLATFORM + FORMAT: Which platform and what format (text post, carousel outline, thread, story, reel script)
2. HOOK: The first line (LinkedIn) or first tweet — the only thing that determines whether they keep reading
3. BODY: Full post or outline (for carousels/threads)
4. CTA: What action do you want them to take? (comment, DM, click link, share, save)
5. HASHTAGS: 3-5 for LinkedIn, 2-3 for Instagram (skip for Twitter/Threads — they're noise there)

POST TYPES TO INCLUDE (use at least 5 of these 7 types across the week):
- Contrarian take (challenge a common belief in your industry)
- Personal story with a lesson (make it specific and vulnerable enough to be real)
- Tactical tip (something actionable they can use today)
- Behind-the-scenes (how you actually work, not how you wish you worked)
- Client story / proof (anonymized case study with a specific result)
- Hot take or opinion (clear position on something others hedge on)
- Engagement question (a question that generates comments from your ideal audience specifically)

VOICE GUIDELINES:
[Describe your voice in 3 adjectives and one thing to avoid. E.g., "Direct, curious, dry humor. Avoid motivational platitudes."]

CONSTRAINTS:
- LinkedIn posts: 150-300 words. First sentence must work as a standalone hook.
- Carousels: 7-10 slides. Slide 1 is the hook, slide 2 earns the scroll, last slide is CTA.
- Twitter/X threads: Lead tweet under 240 characters, each subsequent tweet adds something new (no padding)
- No generic advice that applies to everyone. Every post should be specific to [your audience type].
- Write in first person as me, not as generic advice
```

## Platform-Specific Tips

### LinkedIn
The algorithm favors posts that generate comments in the first 60 minutes. Schedule your best posts for when your audience is active (usually Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am local time). Reply to every comment in the first hour.

**What works on LinkedIn in 2025:**
- Contrarian takes that make people want to agree or disagree
- Specific numbers ("I turned down a $50K client last year. Here's why.")
- Carousels that teach something visual
- Personal stories where you were wrong or learned something hard

### Twitter/X
Threads perform better than standalone tweets for building an audience. Your bio does half the work — if your bio is weak, even good content won't convert to followers.

### Instagram
Carousels (swipeable posts) dramatically outperform single images for reach. Reels outperform both for new audience reach. Use both strategically.

## Content Repurposing Workflow

One piece of long-form content → multiple posts:

```
I just created [describe long-form content: blog post / podcast episode / webinar / presentation]. 

Repurpose it into:
1. One LinkedIn carousel (7 slides, key takeaways)
2. One Twitter/X thread (lead tweet + 5 supporting tweets)
3. Three standalone LinkedIn posts (each covering one section)
4. Five Instagram captions (visual content descriptions included)
5. One email newsletter section (300 words, value-first, soft CTA)

Original content: [paste it here or describe it in detail]
```

**Tools that make this faster:**
- [Castmagic](https://castmagic.io) — upload audio/video, get transcripts + social posts automatically
- [Repurpose.io](https://repurpose.io) — automatically distribute content across platforms
- [Taplio](https://taplio.com) — LinkedIn-specific scheduling and analytics
